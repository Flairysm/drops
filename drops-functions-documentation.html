üìä System Overview

Drops is a modern trading card game application featuring physics-based games (Plinko, Wheel), virtual pack opening mechanics, comprehensive card collection management, and administrative controls. The system uses a 7-tier card rarity system and supports both virtual and physical card management.

üíé Card Tier System (7-Tier)

Tier D (Common)
Most frequent drops - basic cards
~65% probability in standard packs
Tier C (Uncommon)
Moderate rarity cards
~15% probability in standard packs
Tier B (Rare)
Less common drops - valuable cards
~8% probability in standard packs
Tier A (Super Rare)
Valuable drops - sought after cards
~2% probability in standard packs
Tier S (Legendary)
Very rare cards - significant value
~1.5% probability in standard packs
Tier SS (Ultra Legendary)
Extremely rare cards - high market value
~0.14% probability in standard packs
Tier SSS (Mythic)
Rarest tier - ultimate collectibles
~0.01% probability in standard packs
